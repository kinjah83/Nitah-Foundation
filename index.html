Our Impact

Transforming Lives, One Child at a Time

The Nitah Foundation is a public charity (IRC 501(c)(3)) with a primary mission to transform the lives of children and families through comprehensive support in early childhood education, nutrition, breastfeeding promotion, vaccine campaigns, and disaster relief.


We are committed to empowering communities, fostering holistic development, and building a healthier, more resilient future — one family at a time.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

We invest where impact is greatest — in the earliest years of a child's life, and in communities when they need us most.

Early Childhood Education

By investing in early childhood education, we lay the foundation for brighter futures. We believe every child deserves a strong start — empowering young minds, nurturing curiosity, and creating lifelong learners.

Join Us →

Early Childhood Nutrition

Proper nutrition during the formative years is critical to cognitive development, school readiness, and long-term well-being. We set the stage for a lifetime of healthy habits by prioritizing nutrition early.

Donate Now →

Helping Disaster Victims

When disaster strikes, communities need hope and long-term support. By standing with natural disaster victims, we help restore dignity, inspire resilience, and strengthen communities for tomorrow's challenges.

December 2024

Supporting a Widow's Small Business

We donated 100,000 FCFA to Rosette Ghainso — a widow and mother — to revive her small roadside breakfast business. This gift restored her ability to earn income and feed her children with dignity.

December 3, 2020

Course: Teach Kids to Read

We developed a teacher training curriculum and hosted sessions on new techniques for teaching literacy. This empowers teachers to be more effective in classrooms worldwide.

December 3, 2020

School Bus Initiative

Working with local groups, we funded 50 buses and 200 cars to transport children in rural areas to schools around the globe. Transportation is key to education access.

December 3, 2020

10,000 Pencils Delivered

Thanks to our volunteers and generous donors, we delivered 10,000 pencils to children in need — reaching the most underprivileged areas and providing local schools with vital tools.
